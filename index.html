📋 Quick Reference

Home Inspection Photo Editor

Basic Workflow

1
Import Photos — loads fresh batch. Add Photos appends without clearing.
2
Add Cards — click + between photos or Add Card in toolbar. Type group name or ##code into card. Drag to reorder.
3
Click Apply Cards — assigns all captions based on your cards. Always click after arranging cards.
4
Save & Next through photos, then Save All to write files to folder.

The Card System

Group Card — starts a group
Water Heater
📷 Photo 1 → "Water Heater"
📷 Photo 2 → "View 2 - Water Heater"
##000 — close with label only
##000
Closes group using group name as caption — no defect text.
##code — close with defect caption
##605
📷 Photo 1 → "Safety Issue - TPR pipe..."
📷 Photo 2 → "View 2 - Safety Issue..."
Multiple codes — combined caption
##601##604##605
All captions joined with " - ". Any safety code = whole caption turns red + "Safety Issue - " prefix.
Key rule: Every group needs one closing card — either ##000 or a ##code. Ctrl+Z undoes accidental photo deletions.
